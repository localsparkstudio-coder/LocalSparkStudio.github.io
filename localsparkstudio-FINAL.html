FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know. Still have questions? Reach out — we respond the same day.

How long does it take to build my website?
Most sites are done in 5–7 business days from when we receive your content. The Pro package typically takes 7–10 days. Turnaround depends on how quickly you provide feedback and materials.
What information do I need to provide?
We'll need your business name, logo (if you have one), photos, service/page text, contact details, and hours. Don't have everything? We'll walk you through it on our first call — no worries.
Do I need to buy a domain name myself?
We recommend buying your domain through Namecheap or Google Domains (~$10–15/year) so you own it. We handle all the technical setup — connecting it to hosting, SSL certificate, everything.
Do you offer revisions? What if I don't like it?
Yes. Starter Spark includes 1 revision round; Pro includes 3. A round means you review, give feedback, and we make changes. Our goal is that you love the final result.
Will my site look good on phones?
100%. Every site we build is fully mobile-responsive. It automatically adjusts to look great on any screen — phone, tablet, or desktop. More than 60% of local searches happen on mobile, so this is non-negotiable for us.
What's included in the Growth Plan?
The $79/month Growth Plan includes: fast hosting, monthly content updates, uptime monitoring, priority support, Google Business Profile management, and a monthly performance summary.
How does payment work?
Payment is due in full before the final site files are delivered and the site goes live. We accept Zelle, Venmo, PayPal, bank transfer, and card. No hidden fees — what you see is what you pay.
Do you work with businesses outside New Jersey?
Yes — we work with small businesses anywhere in the US, fully remote. Everything is handled via email, phone, and video call. We're based in Sayreville, NJ but our clients come from all across the country.

01 — Scope of Services

What We Do

Local Spark Studio provides web design and development services for small businesses. This includes building new websites, redesigning existing ones, and optional ongoing maintenance through our Growth Plan. Each project is scoped based on the package you select and confirmed before work begins.

02 — Payment

How Payment Works

Payment is due in full before the final website files are delivered and your site goes live. We accept Zelle, Venmo, PayPal, bank transfer, and card.

Simple version: You pay, we deliver. No hidden fees — the price we quote is exactly what you pay.

03 — Revisions

Revision Policy

Each package includes a set number of revision rounds — Starter Spark includes 1 round, Local Spark Pro includes 3. A revision round means you review the work, send feedback, and we make the requested changes. Additional rounds beyond what's included can be arranged for an agreed-upon fee.

04 — Ownership

Who Owns the Site

Once full payment is received, you own the final website — the design, the code, all of it. We will transfer all files to you and you're free to do whatever you want going forward. Before payment, Local Spark Studio retains ownership of all work in progress.

05 — Turnaround

Delivery Timeline

Our quoted timeframes (typically 5–10 business days) are estimates, not guarantees. Delays can occur if we're waiting on content, revisions are extensive, or other circumstances arise. We'll always keep you informed.

06 — Your Responsibilities

What We Need From You

To keep your project on track, please provide the following in a timely manner:

  • Your logo and brand assets (if available)
  • Photos of your business, team, or work
  • Text content for each page (or approval of copy we draft)
  • Feedback on designs within a reasonable timeframe
  • Domain registrar access when needed for setup

We build great websites — but we can't guarantee specific business outcomes like increased revenue, rankings, or customer growth. Results depend on many factors outside our control. Local Spark Studio is not liable for any business losses or outcomes related to your website's performance.

08 — Portfolio Rights

Showcasing Our Work
